Portfolio

Lapy King - LapyKing.com.au

This website was a theme customisation of Wordpress for a computer wholesaler/retailer. The site was divided into the online store (running on Magento) and the informational site and service front end you see below. The site has dynamic forms for collecting service and repair information, and booking service requests. The naviation uses a two tiered menu to drill down to subsections of the website.

Bigtime Records - BigtimeRecords.com

This Californian based record company wanted to showcase its signed roster of artists uncluding news, tours, mechandise and tickets for upcoming shows. It was to be updated by several people and therefore was best designed using a robust CMS. Wordpress was heavily customised for this purpose, turning a blog platform into a standalone website. The brief stated thaat there was to be multiple bands featured news directly on the home page, above the fold. This scrollable, three box design was used to display several articles/posts.

Romeos World - RomeosWorld.net

This is a static standalone website for a book publishing company to promote their children's book club. It included video lighbox and a dynamic colouring book generator that allowed the user to print a PDF with the child's name, age and favourite colour as part of the story.

Daniel Kashuck - DanielKashuck.com

This is again, a Wordpress-based custom site for a Los Angeles musician. It features an image gallery, streaming audio and video, with lazy load and page cache, delivering a large amount of third party content with a relatively fast page render, in a responsive layout.
