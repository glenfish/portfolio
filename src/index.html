Silicon dreams

The first computer I ever laid my hands on was an Osborne 1, the 70's version of a laptop. Then a friend got a Tandy TRS-80. I received my very own Commodore 64 like many other kids in 1984 and I was hooked! So was my grandfather. He taught me to convert decimal to hex when I was just C years old.

I designed my first website in 1999 using scraps of paper & hand-drawn images. I've been developing e-commerce and marketing websites and apps for businesses small and large ever since.
